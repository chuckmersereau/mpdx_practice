You can migrate all your contact information and history from TntConnect into MPDX. Most of your information will import straight into MPDX, including contact info, task history (with notes), notes, user groups, and appeals. MPDX hides contacts with any of the not interested statuses, including not interested and never ask in MPDX. These contacts import in, but do not show up unless you search for hidden contacts.

Export your Database from TntConnect:
  • Click on "File", choose "Utilities" from the list. Then "Maintenance".
  • In the popup box, choose the top button, "Export Database to XML".
  • Then save to your computer.

If you need help, watch this video.

Note: You must have at least TntConnect 3.0. To download the latest version, click here.
